Digital Transformation

Digital transformation is first driven by your business needs and issues. Some of the key questions to address are:
  • What does your business need to prosper in the long-term?
  • Are you able to answer to the growing expectations of your customers?
  • Are you able to adapt to changes, to deliver fast?

    Individual coaching

    Developing high potential employees can be achieved through specific training. However, when there is a need to focus on some specific aspects of the person coaching is quite often a better solution than training.
    Indeed, if you want to inspire the hearts and minds of your employees, go with this different approach: Coach.
    However, the pre-requisite for a successful coaching is the commitment of the employee to go through this process.
    It also requires that the employee will take actions between the sessions according to the mutually agreed plan.
    Trust and confidentiality are key in this process.
    Inputs could be provided by the manager(s) of the employee, potentially his/her peers but no information will be given by the coach unless aprroved by the employee.
    Usually sessions last 1 hour or so over several months so that we can review the progress of the employee and analyze his/her feedback. The first session is to define the objectives, the employee will self-identify and self-direct towards what's missing.

    Let's not forget that stronger leaders create stronger teams, increase employees motivation and performance!
